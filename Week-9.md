### Gaussian Distribution(高斯分布或正态分布)

The formula for the Gaussian density is:
$$
p(x) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}\sigma}\exp\left(-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2\sigma^2}\right)
$$
